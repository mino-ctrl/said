A Love Eternal

Beneath the stars so bright and free,
My heart finds peace, in you, with me.
Like whispered winds through silent night,
Your love surrounds, a gentle light.

In every breath, in every sigh,
You are the reason why I fly.
Our souls entwined, forever bound,
In you, my love, my joy is found.

So here I stand, beneath the sky,
With you forever, love won't die.
Through endless stars, through moonlit streams,
Together we'll chase all our dreams.
